RDA West

West Economic Region covers six municipalities, Istog/Istok, Klinë/Klina, Peja/Peć, Junik, Deçan/Dečani, and Gjakova/Ðakovica. The region is the western gateway to Kosovo bordering Albania and Montenegro (Deçan/Dečani and Peja/Peć Municipalities) and Serbia (Istog/Istok Municipality).

RDA South

Regional Development Agency South (RDA South) operates in the South-Western part of Kosovo. The region has a total area of 2031 km2 (18.66% of the territory of Kosovo and consists of 6 municipalities: Prizren, Dragash/Dragaš, Suhareka/Suvareka, Dragash/Dragaš, Mamusha/Mamuša and Rahovec/Orahovac.

RDA North

Regional Development Agency North (RDA) provides its services to the seven municipalities of Northern Region of Kosovo. Working towards strengthening the Regional Economic Development, RDA North contributes to the vision of the region on achieving high standards of economic and social development while improving its competitiveness, environment and cultural heritage.

RDA East

Regional Development Agency East (RDA East) comprises of eleven municipalities: Ferizaj/Uroševac, Gjilan/Gnjilane, Viti/Vitina, Novobërdë/Novo Brdo, Kamenicë/Kamenica, Hani Elezit/Elez Han, Kaçanik/Kačanik, Shtëpce/Štrpce, Ranillug/Ranilug, Kllokot/Klokot and Partesh/Parteš.

RDA Center

Regional Development Agency – Centre (RDA Centre) is an inter municipal entity founded on December 2008 by the partner municipalities of: Prishtinë/Priština, Drenas/Glogovac, Fushë Kosovë/Kosovo Polje, Obilliq/Obilic, Podujevë/Podujevo and Shtime/Štimlje, Graçanicë/Gracanica and Lipjan/Lipljan.
